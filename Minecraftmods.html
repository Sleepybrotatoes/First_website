Mod Ideas

Here are some ideas for mods that I would like to create in the future:

Mod Idea 1: New Biomes and Creatures

A mod that adds new biomes and creatures to the game to make the world feel more alive and diverse.

Mod Idea 2: New Crafting Recipes and Items

A mod that introduces new crafting recipes and items to the game to enhance the player's experience.

Mod Idea 3: New Dimensions and Portals

A mod that adds new dimensions and portals to the game to allow players to explore new worlds and challenges.

Mod Idea 4: Magic Spells and Abilities

A mod that introduces new magic spells and abilities to the game to give players new ways to interact with the world.
